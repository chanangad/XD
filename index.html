Why it isn't free

An honest, indie unlock

Let's be upfront: this is a paid watch face. The free trial lets you try everything first, and if it sticks, your support keeps it maintained — no ads, no subscriptions, and nothing sold about your data.

  • Pay once, keep it

    A single payment unlocks the watch face for good. There's no recurring charge and nothing to cancel.

  • Pay what feels fair

    Pick any amount from $3. A little extra simply helps fund the next round of features and fixes.

  • Secure checkout

    Payments run through PayPal and Dodo Payments (a merchant of record) over HTTPS. Your card details never touch this site.

  • Restore any time

    New watch or reinstall? Use the same email under Restore access to move your unlock across devices.
